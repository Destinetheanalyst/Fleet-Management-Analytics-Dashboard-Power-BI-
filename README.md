The **Fleet Management Analytics Dashboard** provides a consolidated view of fleet operations, enabling organizations to monitor vehicle usage, fuel consumption, maintenance expenditure, and driver performance through interactive Power BI visuals.  

Designed for operational leaders and analysts, the dashboard improves decision-making by identifying high-cost patterns, detecting inefficiencies, and uncovering optimization opportunities across the fleet lifecycle.

# üß† Strategic Insights & Recommendations  

## **1. Fuel Cost Optimization**
- Standardize GPS-based routing  
- Implement driver fuel performance scoring  
- Negotiate volume-based fuel contracts  
- Consider fleet upgrades to more fuel-efficient models  

---

## **2. Proactive Predictive Maintenance**
- Adopt telematics for real-time asset health monitoring  
- Transition to preventive maintenance targeting expected breakdown cycles  
- Sync maintenance windows with downtime periods (e.g., December)  

---

## **3. Driver Performance Management**
- Introduce a formalized driver scorecard  
- Provide coaching and training to high-cost drivers  
- Optimize vehicle-to-driver assignments  

---

## **4. Operational Optimization**
- Use seasonal trends for workforce and route planning  
- Reallocate workloads to improve driver utilization  
- Investigate unexpected operational drops or surges  

---

## **5. Data Governance & Quality Improvement**
- Enforce proper logging standards to eliminate ‚ÄúNo Driver‚Äù entries  
- Automate fleet data ingestion for accuracy  
- Implement validation rules for fuel and maintenance data
